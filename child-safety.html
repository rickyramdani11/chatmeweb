Child Safety Policy – ChatMe

Effective Date: [Isi tanggal]

ChatMe is committed to providing a safe and secure platform for all users, especially minors. We strictly prohibit any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and enforce a zero-tolerance policy against such content or behavior.

Prohibited Content and Behavior

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or any exploitative content involving minors.
  • Communication with minors that is predatory, harmful, or abusive.
  • Any attempt to engage minors in inappropriate conversations or activities.

Reporting and Enforcement

Users can report abusive or harmful behavior directly in the app. Our moderation team will:

  • Review reports promptly.
  • Remove any violating content or users.
  • Report violations to appropriate authorities when required.
